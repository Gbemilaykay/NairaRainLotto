About NairaRain Lotto (NRL)

NairaRain Lotto (NRL) is a lottery game with a starting jackpot of ₦25,000,000 that rolls over until it is won. Players match five (5) numbers from 1–47 plus 1 NairaBall from 1–10 to win the JACKPOT. There are 10 prize tiers and winnings for matching one (1) main number + 0 NairaBall.

NRL Rapid Games offers daily draws where players can win ₦500,000, ₦250,000, ₦150,000 and other tier prizes by picking 5 lucky numbers from 1–27, 1–25, or 1–21 numbers respectively.

How do I play NairaRain Lotto?

  • Select five (5) lucky numbers from 1–47 + 1 NairaBall from 1–10.
